About PatchMyPDF

We believe that editing PDFs should be fast, free, and above all—secure.

Our Mission

We built PatchMyPDF to fix a basic problem: finding a decent PDF editor that respects your privacy. Most online tools force you to upload your sensitive documents to their servers. We thought there should be a better, safer way.

Privacy First

Our editor runs entirely inside your browser. When you open a PDF, it never leaves your computer. We don't use cloud storage, we don't save your files, and we don't track your data. You maintain total control over your documents.

Free to Use

There are no paywalls, hidden subscriptions, or confusing pricing tiers here. You don't even need to create an account. Just drag, drop, and edit your PDFs for free.

Privacy & Security FAQ

Everything you need to know about how we protect your data.

How does local processing work?
Our editor uses WebAssembly and modern browser APIs to process your PDFs directly on your device. When you open a file, it's loaded into your browser's memory, not our servers. All edits, annotations, and signing happen locally.
Are my files stored anywhere?
No. Your files are never uploaded to any cloud storage or server. They exist only in your browser's temporary memory while the tab is open. Once you close the tab or refresh the page, the data is cleared from memory.
Do you track what's inside my documents?
Absolutely not. Since the processing is 100% local, we have no technical way to see your content, filenames, or metadata. We don't even log that a specific file was opened.
Can I use this tool offline?
Yes! Once the page is loaded, you can disconnect from the internet and continue editing your PDFs. The core logic is bundled within the application and runs entirely in your browser.
